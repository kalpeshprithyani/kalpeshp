Namaste
My name is Kalpesh Prithyani and I am a web designer / developer based in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh.


I have a passion for simple interfaces and love to code. Like all Indians I share the love of Biryani, Samosa and Pani puri! I also say jhakaas.



I am currently studying Computer Science at Institute of Engineering & Technology, Indore. One of the greatest strengths about being a designer with an engineer background is that not only I can handle the graphical aspects of a project, but I can also fully understand, participate and even lead the technical aspects of those same projects.

In this website you will find a selection of projects I have had the pleasure to work on. I am always happy to discuss potential opportunities, and if you have a project you would like to collaborate on I would love to hear from you!

Thank you for visiting and I hope you enjoy your stay!
